About PakPhantom

Made in Pakistan, loved worldwide. Learn our story, mission, and what drives us.

Our Mission

PakPhantom was born out of a simple idea — to create the best multi-purpose Discord bot from Pakistan. We wanted to build something that represents our culture, humor, and community spirit while being packed with features that rival any international bot.

From desi jokes to complex game algorithms, every line of code is written with love and dedication. Our goal is to make every Discord server more fun — regardless of size — with commands that are easy to use, visually beautiful, and completely free.

We believe technology should be accessible. That's why PakPhantom will always remain 100% free — no premium tiers, no paywalls, no hidden costs. Just pure fun for everyone. 💚

📅 Journey

Our Timeline

Key milestones in PakPhantom's development journey.

🌱 Phase 1 - Foundation

Bot Created

PakPhantom was created with basic commands — help, ping, and simple fun commands. The foundation was laid with Discord.js v14 and MongoDB.

🎮 Phase 2 - Games

Game System Added

22 games were developed including Blackjack, Wordle, Snake, Slots, Trivia, Connect4, Minesweeper, and more. AI opponents with multiple difficulty levels.

💕 Phase 3 - Social

Interactions & Marriage

10 interaction commands with GIFs (hug, kiss, slap, etc.) and a complete marriage system with proposals, divorce, and anniversary tracking.

💰 Phase 4 - Economy

Economy System

Full economy system with coins, daily rewards, work commands, shop, and integration with casino games for betting.

🌐 Phase 5 - APIs

API Integrations

15+ external API integrations for weather, animals, anime, movies, facts, dad jokes, and more real-time content.

🚀 Phase 6 - Launch

Public Launch & Website

Official public launch with 94 commands, this website, documentation, and the support community. Made in Pakistan, loved worldwide! 🇵🇰
